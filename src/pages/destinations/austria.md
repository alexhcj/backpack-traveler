<p class="md-paragraph">
  Austria blends <span class="md-text-accent">refined elegance and raw alpine nature</span>. Grand palaces, world-class museums, and snow-covered peaks define the country’s timeless charm.
</p>
<div class="md-space"></div>

<p class="md-paragraph">
  Whether you’re sipping coffee in Vienna or hiking in Tyrol, Austria rewards travelers who appreciate culture, order, and scenic beauty.
</p>
