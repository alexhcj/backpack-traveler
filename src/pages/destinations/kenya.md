<p class="md-paragraph">
  Kenya is a land of dramatic landscapes, rich traditions, and unforgettable wildlife encounters. From the rolling plains of the Maasai Mara to the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, the country offers <span class="md-text-accent">raw beauty and cultural depth</span> at every turn.
</p>
<div class="md-space"></div>

<p class="md-paragraph">
  Traveling through Kenya means early-morning game drives, lively local markets, and evenings spent under star-filled skies. It’s a destination where nature and humanity exist in powerful balance.
</p>
<div class="md-space-xl"></div>

<h3 class="ui-post-title" id="typical-costs">Typical costs when travelling</h3>
<div class="md-space-ms"></div>

<p class="md-paragraph"><b class="md-bold">Accommodation</b> – Budget hostels and guesthouses are affordable, while safari lodges can be costly depending on location and season.</p>
<div class="md-space-s"></div>

<p class="md-paragraph"><b class="md-bold">Food</b> – Local meals are inexpensive and filling. International cuisine costs more in tourist hubs.</p>
<div class="md-space-lg"></div>

<p class="md-paragraph"><b class="md-bold">Transportation</b> – Matatus are cheap but chaotic. Trains and domestic flights are safer for long distances.</p>
