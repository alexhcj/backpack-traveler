<div class="md-space"></div>
<p class="md-paragraph">
  <span class="md-first-letter">C</span>anada’s landscapes tell stories of raw wilderness and timeless beauty. From the glacier-fed lakes of Banff to the vast stretches of untouched tundra in the Yukon, every province offers a new chapter in nature’s grand design. This journey through Canada’s natural wonders reveals why travelers and photographers alike find this country irresistible.
</p>

<div class="md-space-xl"></div>
<h4 class="md-list-title">Top Natural Wonders to Explore</h4>
<div class="md-space-lg"></div>
<ul class="md-list col-1">
  <li class="md-list-item">Banff National Park – Emerald lakes and the Canadian Rockies.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Niagara Falls – The iconic cascade between Canada and the US.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Gros Morne National Park – Dramatic fjords and coastal cliffs in Newfoundland.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">The Northern Lights – A magical dance across Yukon and Northwest Territories skies.</li>
</ul>

<div class="md-space-lg"></div>
<p class="md-paragraph">Traveling through Canada feels like moving through a series of grand paintings — each vista more surreal than the last. Whether you’re hiking through icefields, canoeing past mirrored lakes, or standing beneath the Northern Lights, Canada’s natural wonders invite you to rediscover your sense of awe.</p>
