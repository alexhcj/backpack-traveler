<p class="md-paragraph">
  <span class="md-first-letter">S</span>tretching along Canada’s Pacific coast, Vancouver Island is a wonderland of ocean breezes, towering pines, and hidden beaches. From the surfing town of Tofino to the serene coves of Victoria, the island’s diverse coastline promises endless discovery.
</p>

<div class="md-space-xl"></div>
<h4 class="md-list-title">Top Spots to Explore</h4>
<ul class="md-list col-1">
  <li class="md-list-item">Tofino – Surf, sunsets, and Pacific spirit.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Cathedral Grove – Walk among ancient Douglas firs.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Victoria’s Inner Harbour – A perfect coastal stroll.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Pacific Rim National Park – A true wild escape.</li>
</ul>

<p class="md-paragraph">
  Whether kayaking through calm bays or watching whales breach under orange sunsets, Vancouver Island feels like a world apart. Every turn brings a new scene that captures the soul of Canada’s untamed west.
</p>
