<div class="md-space"></div>
<p class="md-paragraph">
  <span class="md-first-letter">B</span>razil’s coastline stretches for thousands of kilometers, and each beach carries its own rhythm — a mix of natural wonder, local life, and cultural energy. From the samba beats of Rio to the tranquil shores of Bahia, the beaches here are more than destinations — they’re experiences.
</p>

<div class="md-space-xl"></div>
<h4 class="md-list-title">Cultural Highlights by the Sea</h4>
<div class="md-space-lg"></div>
<ul class="md-list col-1">
  <li class="md-list-item">Copacabana, Rio de Janeiro – The world’s most iconic urban beach.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Jericoacoara, Ceará – Sand dunes, lagoons, and a laid-back spirit.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Praia do Forte, Bahia – Where sea turtles meet traditional fishing culture.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Ilha Grande, Rio State – Untouched rainforest meets turquoise waters.</li>
</ul>

<p class="md-paragraph">Beyond the beauty lies culture — local markets, beachside music, and food stalls serving acarajé and fresh coconuts. To experience Brazil’s beaches is to immerse yourself in its soul, where sun, rhythm, and community flow together effortlessly.</p>
