<div class="md-space"></div>
<p class="md-paragraph">
  <span class="md-first-letter">V</span>ienna blends imperial elegance with modern rhythm. In one week, you can experience centuries of art, music, and café culture — all wrapped in the city’s timeless charm.
</p>

<div class="md-space-md"></div>

<p class="md-excerpt">
  Here’s how to spend a perfect week in Austria’s capital — from palaces and waltzes to markets and river walks.
</p>

<div class="md-space-lg"></div>
<h4 class="md-list-title">7-Day Highlights</h4>
<div class="md-space-lg"></div>
<ul class="md-list col-1">
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>Day 1: Schönbrunn Palace & Gardens</b> – Start your journey at the Habsburgs’ summer residence. Stroll through its manicured gardens and visit the Gloriette for panoramic views.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>Day 2: St. Stephen’s Cathedral & the Historic Center</b> – Explore Vienna’s heart — climb the cathedral tower, then wander cobblestone streets lined with cafés and boutiques.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>Day 3: Vienna State Opera & Musical Evenings</b> – Attend a performance or tour this world-famous opera house. Finish the night with classical music at the Musikverein or a local concert hall.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>Day 4: MuseumsQuartier & Local Art</b> – Discover modern art at the Leopold Museum or MUMOK, surrounded by courtyard cafés and design shops.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>Day 5: Danube River Walk & Prater Park</b> – Enjoy nature in the city. Take a riverside stroll or a spin on the historic Ferris wheel for a view of old and new Vienna.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>Day 6: Coffeehouse Crawl</b> – Experience Vienna’s café culture — try Café Central, Demel, and Café Sperl for cakes, history, and quiet reflection.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>Day 7: Naschmarkt & Farewell Feast</b> – Browse fresh produce, spices, and street food stalls at the Naschmarkt — a flavorful finale to your Viennese adventure.</li>
</ul>

<p class="md-paragraph">
  Vienna is a city of rhythm — not just in music, but in the pace of daily life. Between imperial façades and quiet courtyards, time seems to slow, inviting you to listen, taste, and breathe in the elegance that defines Austria’s capital.
</p>

<div class="md-space-lg"></div>

<blockquote class="md-blockquote">
  “In Vienna, art and coffee are equally sacred — both meant to be savored slowly.”
</blockquote>

<p class="md-paragraph">
  A week in Vienna leaves a lifetime of memories — symphonies echoing through grand halls, the scent of apple strudel, and golden sunsets over the Danube. Take your time, walk slowly, and let Vienna reveal itself in layers of history and grace.
</p>
