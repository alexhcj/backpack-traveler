<p class="md-paragraph">
  <span class="md-first-letter">P</span>aris — the City of Light — has inspired artists, dreamers, and travelers for centuries. With grand boulevards, riverside cafés, and timeless landmarks, this guide helps you experience the real Paris — elegant yet effortless, romantic yet grounded in everyday life.
</p>

<div class="md-space-md"></div>

<p class="md-excerpt">Discover the top 5 neighborhoods, iconic attractions, and local secrets — all mapped for the perfect Paris adventure.</p>

<div class="md-space-lg"></div>

<h4 class="md-list-title">Top 5 Neighborhoods to Explore</h4>
<div class="md-space-md"></div>
<ul class="md-list col-1">
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>1. Le Marais</b> – Trendy boutiques, Jewish bakeries, and art galleries in cobblestone streets.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>2. Montmartre</b> – Home of Sacré-Cœur, artists’ studios, and panoramic city views.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>3. Saint-Germain-des-Prés</b> – Literary cafés, jazz bars, and vintage bookshops.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>4. Latin Quarter</b> – Student life, the Sorbonne, and hidden bistros near the Seine.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item"><b>5. 7th Arrondissement</b> – Elegant boulevards and, of course, the Eiffel Tower.</li>
</ul>

<h4 class="md-list-title">Top 5 Must-See Attractions</h4>
<div class="md-space-md"></div>
<ul class="md-list col-1">
  <li class="md-list-item">Eiffel Tower – best at sunrise or sparkling after dark.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Louvre Museum – home of Mona Lisa and endless galleries.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Notre-Dame Cathedral – under restoration, but its beauty endures.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Musée d’Orsay – Impressionist art in a grand railway station.</li>
  <li class="md-list-item">Seine River Cruise – a gentle way to see the city glow.</li>
</ul>
